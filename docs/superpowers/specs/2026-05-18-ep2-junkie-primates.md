## The Five Mechanisms

EP.2 names five interlocking mechanisms. None is novel — each is empirically grounded in mainstream science. The novelty is the substrate-honest application: naming them together, without moralizing, as the *combined* explanation for the current condition.

### 1. Self-domestication (Wrangham 2019, Hare 2017)

Humans selected each other for reduced aggression over ~250,000 years through cooperative-bullying enforcement: groups executed or expelled bullies, leaving the cooperative-tame to reproduce. The genetic signature is comparable to dog-vs-wolf domestication — smaller brain, reduced sexual dimorphism, retained juvenile features, increased social tolerance.

**Implication:** humans are not "wild" primates with bad habits. They are *domesticated* primates with the inheritance the domestication produced — both the cooperative capacities AND the dependencies on social embedding that domestication requires.

### 2. Hypernormal stimuli (Tinbergen 1951, Barrett 2010)

Evolved reward systems calibrate to ancestral environment. Modern environments offer supernormal versions of every evolved cue: refined sugar (vs. seasonal fruit), pornography (vs. seasonal mating), social media metrics (vs. small-group status), processed food (vs. forage), gambling (vs. uncertain reward gradients), opioid pharmaceuticals (vs. endogenous endorphins).

**Implication:** craving is not weakness. The reward circuit is doing exactly what evolution selected for — pursuing the strongest available reward signal. The signal is engineered to be stronger than anything evolution ever produced. "Junkie primates" names this descriptively: the same neural architecture that drove foraging primates to find ripe fruit drives modern primates to scroll feeds for 7 hours a day.

### 3. WEIRD-niche outrun (Henrich 2020)

Modern Western Educated Industrialized Rich Democratic societies are a recent evolutionary niche. Most evolved psychological adaptations were calibrated to small-group (~150-person), kin-based, low-mobility, high-trust environments. The WEIRD niche has none of those properties.

**Implication:** loneliness, atomization, low fertility, anxiety, and the felt-sense of "the world is wrong" are not personal failures or moral defects. They are predictable outputs of running an evolved psychology in a niche it was not calibrated for.

### 4. Sexual-selection asymmetry at scale (Y-chromosome bottleneck data; modern dating-app structure)

The Y-chromosome bottleneck (~5,000–7,000 years ago: 17 women reproduced per 1 man) shows extreme reproductive asymmetry is the historical default, not the exception. Modern dating-app data (Tinder Gini 0.58; top 1% of men receive 16%+ of female swipes) shows the same mathematical structure resurfacing once cultural compression (marriage as universal contract) collapses.

**Implication:** the male loneliness epidemic, the rise of incel ideology, the female-side dissatisfaction with mating-market quality, and the broader "battle of the sexes" rhetoric are not separate problems. They are facets of one underlying asymmetry that culture had previously compressed and that modern affordances (dating apps, urbanization, female economic independence) have decompressed.

This is the load-bearing case [ALETHEIA](/Users/macair/Desktop/ALETHEIA/) details. EP.2 names it at the macro level; ALETHEIA names it in depth.

### 5. Information environment outrun by evolution

The brain's threat-detection, in-group/out-group, status-monitoring, and pattern-completion circuits evolved for environments with ~150 known persons, ~3 hours of social novelty per day, and zero broadcast media. The current environment offers billions of persons, infinite social novelty, and 24/7 broadcast. Tribalism, conspiracy thinking, ideological capture, and political polarization are the predictable output of running this hardware on this input.

**Implication:** people are not stupid or evil for falling into ideology. The hardware is doing exactly what it was selected to do — find tribe, defend tribe, pattern-match threat. The input is engineered to maximize each output.
