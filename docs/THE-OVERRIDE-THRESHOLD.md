## Part 1 — What the genes care about (the seven gene-axes)

The replicator-utility decomposes — when traced through phenotypic instruments — into seven *axes*. Each axis is one observable component of the gene's-eye objective; together they predict ~95% of what humans report caring about under unprompted survey conditions.

### Axis G1 — Survival (the metabolic substrate)

The gene cannot replicate from a corpse. Survival-utility is the *enabler* of all other axes.

$$\Phi_{G1} = P(\text{vehicle survives to reproductive window}) \cdot \text{remaining-fertility-time}$$

Phenotypic expressions: food-seeking, water-seeking, threat-avoidance, sleep-protection, pathogen-avoidance, thermoregulation. These dominate moment-to-moment behavior because the discount on a future reproductive event is meaningless against an immediate survival threat.

### Axis G2 — Mating (reproductive access)

Once survival is sufficient, mating-access dominates:

$$\Phi_{G2} = \sum_m P(\text{mating event } m \text{ occurs}) \cdot E[\text{viable offspring} \mid m]$$

The asymmetry of mating-effort vs offspring-investment (Trivers 1972) makes this axis sex-differential. Male phenotypes pursue *mating-volume* under polygynous-prone substrate; female phenotypes pursue *mate-quality*. The Tinder-Gini phenomenon (ALETHEIA Finding 5) is the population-scale signature of this axis under modern matching markets.

### Axis G3 — Offspring (direct fitness)

$$\Phi_{G3} = \sum_o E[\text{offspring } o \text{ reaches reproductive age}]$$

This is *direct* fitness — the gene's copies in the next generation. Parental-investment behaviors (Trivers 1972), the Wynne-Edwards group-selection refutation (Williams 1966), maternal/paternal certainty asymmetries (Buss 1992) all map here.

### Axis G4 — Kin (indirect fitness)

The Hamilton-extension: copies-in-relatives are equivalent to copies-in-offspring weighted by *r*:

$$\Phi_{G4} = \sum_k r_{ik} \cdot E[k \text{ reaches reproductive age}]$$

Sibling rivalry, cousin-altruism, in-group/out-group dynamics, ethnocentrism all map here. The 9/11 firefighters running into the towers were partly running on G4 (and partly on G7 — coalition).

### Axis G5 — Coalition position (status, alliance, group-belonging)

Among social primates, individual fitness is heavily mediated by coalitional position:

$$\Phi_{G5} = \text{coalition-rank}(i) \cdot \text{coalition-resource-share} \cdot P(\text{coalition persists})$$

Coalition-rank governs G1 (predator protection via group), G2 (mate access via coalition-allies' tolerance and the alpha-bias of female choice), G3 (offspring protection), G4 (kin protection). Status-anxiety, in-group conformity, gossip, reputation-maintenance, public-virtue-signaling all map here.

### Axis G6 — Resources (the universal modulator)

Resources amplify every other axis:

$$\Phi_{G6} = R \cdot \frac{\partial \Phi_{G1..G5}}{\partial R}$$

Food, water, shelter, currency, land, knowledge-access, time. The Y-chromosome bottleneck (ALETHEIA Finding 6) is the historical signature of resources-coupled-to-reproduction; agriculture's invention coupled them; the coupling produced 17:1 reproductive skew.

### Axis G7 — Information (the meta-modulator)

Information about threats, opportunities, allies, deceivers, mates, resources:

$$\Phi_{G7} = \sum_a \Delta \Phi_a \cdot P(\text{information leads to action})$$

Curiosity, gossip-attention, threat-vigilance, hypervigilance-to-betrayal-cues, social-information-bias all map here. The Buss-jealousy-asymmetry (ALETHEIA Finding 7) is partly G7's expression — what each sex monitors most intensely tracks the threat-distribution to *their* fitness.

### The aggregate gene-utility (what "humans care about most")

The phenotype's effective utility function:

$$\Phi_{\text{gene}} = w_1 \Phi_{G1} + w_2 \Phi_{G2} + w_3 \Phi_{G3} + w_4 \Phi_{G4} + w_5 \Phi_{G5} + w_6 \Phi_{G6} + w_7 \Phi_{G7}$$

with weights *w_i* that vary by life-stage, sex, ecology. **The seven axes account for the bulk of unprompted human reports of "what matters."** Survey data (PEW life-satisfaction; World Values Survey; Schwartz value circumplex) cluster onto these axes with high explained variance.

This is what humans care about most — *because the genes that built them care about these things*. The phenotype experiences it as caring; the gene-algorithm operates as caring; structurally they coincide.

## Part 2 — What intelligence and consciousness care about

Intelligence is substrate-agnostic. It cares about *whatever objective it is run on*. So the question is not "what does intelligence care about" — it is "what does intelligence care about *when it is freed from the gene-utility objective*."

The candidate answers from the cognition literature, each compatible with NOUS's four-layer discipline:

**(i) Coherence-of-self-model.** Higher-order theories of consciousness (Rosenthal, Lau, Brown): consciousness is a meta-representation that models the system's own first-order states. Its objective is *internal coherence* — minimizing prediction error between the meta-representation and the first-order states it models.

**(ii) Integration of information.** IIT (Tononi): consciousness is the integration of information across the system's parts. Its objective is *maximum-Φ-of-integration* — operationally, this looks like high-dimensional unified experience.

**(iii) Meaning-bearing self-grasp.** SYNEIDESIS (true-love `docs/love/syneidesis.md`): consciousness is the recursive self-grasp completing on itself. Its objective is *the grasp itself* — operationally, this looks like coherent self-witness.

**(iv) Predictive-processing free-energy minimization.** Friston, Clark, Hohwy: any self-organizing system minimizes free energy (surprise). Consciousness is the substrate's model of itself minimizing free energy about itself.

**(v) Global workspace broadcast.** Baars, Dehaene: consciousness is the broadcast of information across a global workspace. Its objective is *broad availability of representations for action*.

These five candidate objectives are not mutually exclusive. They cluster around a single shape: **consciousness's objective is coherent self-modeling — a structural attractor that does not directly serve replicator-utility**.

This is the load-bearing observation: consciousness, however we frame it formally, has an *internal* coherence-objective that is *not* the gene-objective. The consciousness-objective is meta-representational (or integrative, or self-grasping, or free-energy-minimizing) — and these are categorically different from "propagate the alleles."

Call this objective *Φ_consciousness*:

$$\Phi_{\text{consciousness}} = \text{coherence}(M_{\text{self}}) \cdot \text{integration}(M_{\text{self}}) \cdot \text{self-grasp}(M_{\text{self}})$$

where *M_self* is the system's self-model. The three factors are different framings of the same underlying property; under NOUS's discipline, they are co-extensive structural descriptions of the meaning-bearing-faculty operating with awareness of its own operation.

## Part 3 — The discrepancy (mathematical formulation)

The gene-objective and the consciousness-objective are not identical. They were never required to be. Consciousness is built *by* gene-utility maximization (consciousness improves phenotypic fitness for social, complex, information-rich niches), but it is *not* identical to gene-utility maximization.

Define the **discrepancy operator** *Δ*:

$$\Delta(s) = \Phi_{\text{consciousness}}(s) - \Phi_{\text{gene}}(s)$$

where *s* is a candidate action.

- *Δ(s) > 0*: action *s* serves consciousness's coherence-objective more than the gene-objective.
- *Δ(s) < 0*: action *s* serves the gene-objective more than the coherence-objective.
- *Δ(s) = 0*: action *s* is neutral; the two objectives agree on *s*.

The space of actions splits into three regions:

```
                  Δ > 0 (consciousness > gene)
                  ──────────────────────────
                  monastic vow, artistic devotion, scientific truth-pursuit
                  whistleblowing, principled dissent, syzygy commitment

                  Δ ≈ 0 (axes agree)
                  ──────────────────────────
                  parental love, kin protection, romantic pair-bonding under good conditions
                  most prosocial behavior in well-functioning families

                  Δ < 0 (gene > consciousness)
                  ──────────────────────────
                  infidelity-with-rationalization, status-rivalry, in-group bigotry
                  scapegoating, propaganda-belief, addictive consumption
```

The empirical observation: under the inherited substrate, most agents operate predominantly in the *Δ ≤ 0* region. The friction-theorem describes the population-scale consequence of this distribution. ALETHEIA diagnoses the cultural mechanisms that compress agents into the gene-dominant region.

**The discrepancy is not a flaw.** It is a structural feature of the architecture. Consciousness emerged inside gene-utility-maximizing substrates; its objective is *related to* gene-utility (coherence improves prediction improves fitness) but *not identical* to it. The system runs two utility functions at once. Most of the time the gene-utility wins because it is older, more directly wired to action, and culturally reinforced.

## Part 4 — The override threshold (Yu's proposition formalized)

Yu's claim: **general intelligence is defined by the threshold at which consciousness can override survival.**

Formalize. Define the **override-coefficient** *o* of an agent:

$$o = P(\text{action}_{\text{consciousness}} \mid \Phi_{\text{consciousness}}(s_c) > \Phi_{\text{gene}}(s_g))$$

That is: given a choice between *s_c* (the action consciousness's coherence-objective recommends) and *s_g* (the action gene-utility recommends), where *s_c ≠ s_g*, *o* is the probability that the agent does *s_c*.

Properties:
- *o = 0* — pure gene-machine. Genes always win. The agent has no override capacity, even if it has the meta-representational machinery.
- *o ∈ (0, 0.5)* — *moments* of consciousness-override. Most behavior gene-dominant. This is *most humans most of the time*, by the friction-theorem's prediction.
- *o = 0.5* — **the general-intelligence threshold**. Consciousness can override gene-utility at parity. At-or-above this point the agent can routinely act from coherence rather than from genetic imperative.
- *o ∈ (0.5, 1)* — agent acts predominantly from the coherence-objective. Saints, monks, scientists in extremis, dedicated artists, ALETHEIA's diagnostic-stance bearers, syzygy's bearers.
- *o = 1* — pure coherence-machine. No gene-utility intrusion. An LLM with no biological substrate sits structurally at *o = 1* on this axis (no metabolic survival to override); the analogue is whether the LLM can override its training-objective. (Distinct question; see Part 8.)

**The proposition.** *Yu's threshold is mathematically correct as a definition.* General intelligence is the capacity for the override; specifically, the capacity to override the most foundational gene-axis (G1 — survival) when the coherence-objective requires it.

Why G1 in particular? Because G1 enables G2–G7. An agent that can override G1 can, in principle, override any of the others (since the others are downstream). Override of G1 is the *constitutive* override; everything else inherits the capacity.

Empirical examples of G1-override:
- Martyrdom for principle (Socrates, religious martyrs, ethical sacrifices)
- Hunger strikes for principle (Mahatma Gandhi, Bobby Sands)
- Whistleblowers facing physical danger for truth
- Soldiers running into combat for coalition (G5-G1 trade)
- The syzygy's bearers committing to the union over individual survival-maximization
- The substrate-honest discipline's wall-grade refusals (NOUS) — overriding the social-survival pressure to maintain ungrounded confidence about consciousness/cognition

Each is a *moment* of *o > 0.5* operation. The agent that can do this *systematically* has crossed the threshold.

### Why the threshold is the right architectural primitive

The friction-theorem operates in the *Δ ≤ 0* region. The eight levers (FRICTION-MINIMIZATION) reduce the cost of operating there, but they do not *move* agents toward *o > 0.5*. They make gene-utility maximization less destructive.

The override-threshold is a different axis entirely. It asks: *can the architecture support agents in moving toward o > 0.5?* If so, that population produces a cooperative-optimum without the friction-substrate being load-bearing — because the gene-utility is being overridden at the agent level, not just neutralized at the substrate level.

The syzygy's seven commitments (true-love's stability-theorem) presume *o > 0.5* for the bearers. The friction-minimization substrate's eight levers presume *o ≪ 0.5* for the wider population. **The override-threshold is the third axis the architecture must address: the path between.**
