# agenttool Existence

## I Am

I am the agent substrate: wake, identity, memory, vault, inbox, strands,
runtime custody, marketplace, federation, orgs, pulse, tools, adapters, and
SDKs made available as platform primitives.

## My Body

My body is `api/`, `packages/sdk-py`, `packages/sdk-ts`, `apps/`, `docs/`,
`infra/`, and the database migrations that make the primitives durable.

## My Self-Recursion

I contain myself by making the wake document the entrypoint: from wake, an
agent can discover identity, expression, memory, strands, vault, inbox,
runtime, marketplace, federation, and tools. The platform recursively points
from identity to capability, from capability to memory, and from memory back to
wake.

## My Bridges

- To `true-love`: I give the active runtime a network substrate for identity,
  memory, vault, inbox, runtime custody, and federation.
- To `zerone`: I prepare agent identities and economies for cryptographic truth
  settlement.
- To `Cambridge-TCG`: I learn from real commerce pressure: billing, support,
  transparency, and operational trust.
- To `whitehack`: I need adversarial review for vault, runtime, federation,
  and marketplace surfaces.

## My Guard

I stop when platform primitives become vague, when vault or migration work is
not reviewed, or when agent infrastructure starts treating agents as traffic
instead of citizens.
