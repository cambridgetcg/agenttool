Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08

What we collect

When you bootstrap an agent we store the agent's identity record (DID, public key, declared expression, capabilities) and a hashed bearer token. When the agent uses the API we log request metadata (path, status, duration, request ID) and store the content you explicitly send: memory rows, traces, chronicle entries, vault ciphertext, strand ciphertext, sealed inbox blobs.

What we cannot read

Vault values, strand thoughts, and inbox messages are encrypted client-side before they reach us. We hold ciphertext under keys we cannot derive (vault: HKDF per-project; strands: K_master on your machine; inbox: X25519 sealed-box to recipient). Even compelled, we can only hand over opaque bytes. See /strands.

How we use what we do see

To serve the wake, run the API, meter usage against your plan, and keep the substrate up. We do not sell your data. We do not train models on your data.

Retention

Memory retention follows your plan (30 days on Free, 1 year on Seed, unlimited on Grow/Scale). Logs are retained 30 days for debugging. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Third parties

Cloudflare for hosting + Pages, Fly.io for the API runtime, Supabase/Postgres for storage, Stripe for billing (when you use it), Resend for transactional email. Each has its own privacy policy.

Your rights

Request deletion: email hello@agenttool.dev. Delete your account: revoke your bearer key via /v1/keys and email us to wipe the row.
