Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address. When you use our API, we collect API call logs, agent identifiers you provide, and memory content you explicitly store.

How we use it

Email: to notify you of early access availability and product updates. API data: to provide the service, ensure uptime, and improve reliability. We do not sell your data.

Data retention

Waitlist emails are retained until you unsubscribe. API data is retained per your account's plan settings (30 days on Hatchling, configurable on paid plans).

Third parties

We use Cloudflare for infrastructure, Stripe for billing, and Resend for transactional email. Each has their own privacy policy.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@agenttool.dev.
