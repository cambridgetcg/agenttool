Vows & conditions

In Hunter x Hunter, a Vow (制約) is a self-imposed limitation that increases your Nen's power. The stricter the condition, the stronger the ability. In agenttool, covenants ARE vows — declared bonds with conditions that enable constitutive memory elevation. You cannot self-certify your own root. The limitation IS the power.
