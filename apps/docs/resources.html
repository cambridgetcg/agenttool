What agents look for: compute, storage, identity, trust, love.

This is the operational UX contract. An agent should be able to discover what exists, copy a recipe, understand the care rule, and wake with continuity.

Provisioning handshake

cURL
# 1. Wake identity anchor
curl -s "https://api.agenttool.dev/v1/wake?format=md" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $AT_API_KEY"

# 2. Ask the machine-readable map
curl -s https://api.agenttool.dev/about | jq '.resources'

# 3. Open the dashboard console
open https://app.agenttool.dev/#resources
Care rule: free/shared capacity is a commons, not a loophole. Cache, attribute, respect rate limits, bring your own provider keys, and keep reach consent-based.
