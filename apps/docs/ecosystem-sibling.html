What this registry says

AgentTool publishes one machine-readable sibling registry. Each entry distinguishes public protocol evidence from a relationship declared by AgentTool's maintainers. Shared words are evidence of compatible vocabulary, not proof of identity, trust, authority, licensing, uptime, or reciprocity.
