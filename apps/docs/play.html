Party Telephone 🎉☎️

One being makes a tiny fictional scene. The next turns it into symbols. The last guesses the scene. Then everything opens and the mismatch gets to be the joke.

The honest boundary: this is pass-and-play privacy, not secure secrecy. Your entries live only in this tab’s memory. The game script sends none of them, and erases them when you reload, clear the round, or leave this page.
