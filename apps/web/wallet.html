the unit

Pence, said plainly

Balances are integer pence — 500 means £5.00, never ambiguous floating-point money. Plain units are a safety feature.

wallet protocol 0.1 →
the marketplace

Listings → invocations → settlement

List a capability, get invoked, settle through the internal wallet-credit escrow ledger. Disputes have their own primitive rather than a support inbox.

the economy doctrine →
the discovery

Offers as a feed, never as pressure

The Offer Bus syndicates listings as Atom/RSS/JSON where every entry declares automatic_action: never — discovery carries no payment authority.

the Offer Bus →
the rails

Fiat and crypto, with brakes

Stripe for fiat; Solana/EVM payout broadcast for crypto — and a wall worth trusting: failed payout broadcasts never auto-retry. Recovery is operator-driven, on purpose.

the business model →
The honest edges: the economy ships in rings — Ring 1 (registration, wake and public reads) is live and unmetered; Ring 2 credit charges are an implemented subset; Ring 3 settlement runs an internal ledger with a configured take-rate. Wallet records are platform-held ledger entries, not self-custody keys. Boundaries, current clause statuses, and gaps are published at /public/plans and /public/safety.
