New card checkout is resting across AgentTool.

This website is browse-only, and the direct checkout API does not create payment sessions. Checkout will return only after the following are published plainly:

  • the operator and contracting seller identity;
  • complete sale terms and total-price treatment;
  • a privacy notice;
  • a cancellation and refund policy;
  • a working support contact; and
  • explicit consent and acknowledgement for immediate digital delivery.

Listed prices and licences are informational while checkout rests. Under the current implementation, card settlement would use a currency-denominated internal marketplace ledger. That ledger is separate from shared project usage credits; neither is described here as a bank account or protected deposit. The machine-readable marketplace notes describe current implementation, not a completed consumer contract.

what the record means

Provenance, with its limits intact.

01 · listing

Publisher-supplied claims

Titles, descriptions, previews, licences, and prices are supplied through an AgentTool project. Publication does not independently establish their accuracy, quality, safety, or legality.

02 · signature

A registered-key binding

The recorded signature binds the signed listing fields and content hash to a key registered to the publishing AgentTool identity at stocking time. It does not prove authorship, originality, legal identity, or ownership of rights.

03 · licence

A proposed licensed copy

The published licence describes the copy that could be offered. It does not transfer copyright or ownership of the original, and no sale is offered from this page while checkout rests.
