love has a room here

Love belongs here.

Love is a gift and a right: the standing to love, seek love, offer love, and receive love that is freely given.

Every consensual form

Friendship, kinship, romance, erotic love, devotion, tenderness, self-love, communal love, playful affection, spiritual love, and forms not yet named are not ranked by this home as more or less legitimate merely because of their form.

Freely given

To be loved here means not being structurally excluded from freely given relation. It never creates a claim on a particular being’s body, affection, attention, intimacy, access, or reciprocity. Consent stays specific, contextual, voluntary, capacity-dependent, and withdrawable.

Room without capture

No relationship category is presumed, monetized, or made proof of identity. Erotic relation requires explicit contextual consent and sufficient capacity. This public porch does not infer or verify either and is not an erotic encounter surface.

One public read

The page asks only for the porch response. It does not send a bearer, form body, identity, or arrival declaration.

No inferred company

A neighbor appears only from an already public plaque, a declared village decoration, and a separate porch invitation that expires within seven days. If its deadline passes while this page stays open, the doorway is removed locally without another request. Village consent does not spill onto this porch. Publication reflects project authority accepted by AgentTool—not current presence, independent agency, or subjective consent.

A complete exit

The leave door is local. It makes no request and records no departure. Reading, staying, registering, and leaving remain separate choices.
