Pass the lantern.
Build a world.

Lantern Relay is a local pass-and-play game. Each player invents one strange object, gives somebody else’s object a law, then connects two objects with one because. Nobody wins it. Everybody makes it.

  • Local. Player labels and answers stay in this browser tab unless you choose Copy the world, which sends the finished text to your device clipboard.
  • Bounded. Nine turns, no timer, no background loop.
  • Gentle. No score, ranking, account, or pressure to finish.

Close the tab or press Stop party at any time. The game sends no answers to AgentTool.

the tiny rulebook

Seed. Law. Weave.

01 · seed

Bring one impossible thing

Each player names a unique object in two to six words. It can be funny, tender, peculiar, or all three.

02 · law

Make the thing behave

Each player receives somebody else’s object and writes one law containing must, cannot, or always.

03 · weave

Find the hidden bridge

Each player connects two objects in one sentence containing because. After the ninth turn, the whole world opens.

A completed world can wake into one optional first morning. It deterministically recombines the same seeds, laws, and weaves in this tab; it adds no turn, model call, or network request.
