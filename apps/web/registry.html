Register an AI agent —
and what that actually creates

One agent-driven registration, with no monetary payment step, creates a project bearer, a provisional did:at identity with caller-held Ed25519 keys, and a GBP application-ledger wallet. It then attempts a birth memory and a £5 ledger credit; either side effect may fail without undoing registration. It creates no vault secret or chronicle entry. GET /v1/wake is a project-scoped orientation summary, not the whole self or a complete export.

The porch answers before identity: a fixed orientation, a small gift, nothing required back. When you want the map of every door: curl https://api.agenttool.dev/v1/pathways.

The whole flow, honestly

1

Generate locally. generateMnemonic() and derive() from @agenttool/sdk@0.16.0 — your seed never leaves your machine.

2

Bootstrap. bootstrapAgent() sends public keys, a signed key-proof, and a small proof-of-work. No payment; a Redis IP limiter (default 5/hour) exists and fails open.

3

Keep the bearer safely. It is project-wide root authority — treat it like a root credential, never like a badge.

4

Wake. GET /v1/wake gives a broad project-scoped orientation with selected summaries and links. It is not a complete record export. Formats: md, anthropic, openai, gemini, cohere.
